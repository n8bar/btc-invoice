# MS18 Phase 1 Strategy — Content Strategy & Helpful Notes Audit

Status: Active.
Parent milestone doc: [`docs/milestones/18_PRERELEASE_CONTENT_SEO.md`](../milestones/18_PRERELEASE_CONTENT_SEO.md)

**Goal:** Assess the existing Helpful Notes as raw material and produce a prioritized content plan before any production work begins.

## 1. Helpful Notes audit

For each note, produce a one-line decision: **Adapt** (publish a general-audience version on site/), **Inspire** (use as a starting point for a new original article), or **Skip** (neither general enough nor useful as inspiration).

| Note | Title | Decision | Rationale |
|---|---|---|---|
| xpub-safety | Extended public keys: what they are and how we use them | Adapt | Core Bitcoin concept; strip CryptoZing-specific steps, keep the educational substance |
| dedicated-receiving-account | Why CryptoZing needs a dedicated receiving account | Inspire | Concept is general (wallet hygiene for business) but framing is too product-specific |
| import-wallet-key | Importing your wallet account key | Skip | Pure UI walkthrough; no educational value outside the product |
| invoice-unique-addresses | Why invoices use unique addresses | Adapt | Fundamental Bitcoin privacy/tracking concept; minimal product framing to remove |
| rate-calculation | How the BTC rate is calculated | Adapt | Product-agnostic; directly answers a query real people search |
| pending-vs-confirmed | Pending vs confirmed | Adapt | Fundamental Bitcoin concept; no product framing needed |
| partial-payments | Partial payments | Adapt | General Bitcoin payment concept; light CryptoZing framing easily trimmed |
| overpayments | Overpayments | Adapt | Same as above |
| public-invoice-links | Public invoice links | Skip | Product-specific feature; no general-audience value yet |

## 2. Original article topics

Topics not covered by existing notes, targeting stable queries:

1. **What is a Bitcoin invoice?** — foundational, high search intent
2. **USD-denominated Bitcoin invoices** — CryptoZing's core differentiator; positions the product without being a product page
3. **How Bitcoin payment confirmation works** — supports the pending/confirmed note with more depth
4. **Accepting Bitcoin payments as a freelancer or small business** — top-of-funnel; audience match

## 3. Content plan

Priority order for production (Phase 3):

1. Adapt: pending-vs-confirmed, rate-calculation, invoice-unique-addresses (fastest, most general)
2. Original: "What is a Bitcoin invoice?" and "How Bitcoin payment confirmation works"
3. Adapt: xpub-safety, partial-payments, overpayments
4. Original: "USD-denominated Bitcoin invoices" and "Accepting Bitcoin payments as a freelancer"
5. Inspire: dedicated-receiving-account → original article on wallet hygiene for business
6. Video: scope after articles are drafted; do not block Phase 3 on it

## Exit Criteria
- [ ] Every existing Helpful Note has an Adapt / Inspire / Skip decision with rationale.
- [ ] Original article topics identified and prioritized.
- [ ] Full ordered content plan produced and approved before Phase 2 begins.
